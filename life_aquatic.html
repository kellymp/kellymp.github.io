About

The film follows Oceanographer Steve Zissou as he films a documentary about exacting revenge upon the rare and elusive Jaguar Shark that killed his partner, Esteban. The film takes place aboard Zissou's ship, The Belafonte.

Fun Facts

  • Oceanographer Jacques-Yves Cousteau was the inspiration behind the film
  • The Belafonte crew uniforms were inspired by the uniforms from Star Trek
  • Stop motion animation was to used to create all the scenes with the underwater animals
  • The character Jane Winslett-Richardson was based on anthrpologist, Jane Goodall.
  • A lawyer named Steve Zissou negotiated with the movie studio to use his name for Murray's character. The following text appears in the end credits: "The filmmakers acknowledge that the real Steve Zissou is a prominent attorney in New York City specializing in complex federal litigation."
