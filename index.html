About

Wes Anderson is an American film director and producer. Many of his films have been nominated for Academy Awards, and in 2014 he won a Golden Globe Award for his film, The Grand Budapest Hotel. His films are known for their:

  • Highly controlled color palettes
  • Use of symmetry
  • Focus on architectural elements
  • Reoccuring use of the font Futura
  • An illustrative story book quality
  • Casting of reoccuring actors (Ex: Bill Murray)

Fun Facts

  • Many characters are named after his high school classmates
  • Each of his film's has a references to Charlie Brown
  • He makes cameos in several of his films
