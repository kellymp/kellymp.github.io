About the Film

In the film, M. Gustave, the concierge of the Grand Budapest Hotel is framed for the murder of an elderly hotel patron. With the help of his bell boy, Zero he sets out to prove his innocence but meets several setbacks along the way.

Fun Facts

  • It is Anderson's highest grossing film
  • The film won 4 Academy Awards
  • Prada created one-of-a-kind luggage pieces that were used in the film
  • Anderson wrote all the content for the newspapers that flash on the screen throughout the film
  • Anderson comissioned an artist to create the Painting Boy with Apple specifically for the film
